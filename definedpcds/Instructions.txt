Instructions for creating database point.
1. Press ctrl del after the red part to del unwanted parts
2. Use translate center set origin to move more or less to center
3. rotate about x, -30 deg
4. Y axis 90 degree
5. Z axis 180 degrees, this aligns up the axis definitions correctly.
~ X to the right, y up and z towards the viewer
6. lastly,translate the origin to the tip of the cone.
7. Roughly there and then save the ply.
